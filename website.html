📖 Book of the Month

The Midnight Library

by Matt Haig

Between life and death there is a library, and within that library, the shelves go on forever. Every book provides a chance to try another life you could have lived. To see how things would be if you had made other choices... Would you have done anything different, if you had the chance to undo your regrets?

Why You Should Read This:

  • Thought-provoking exploration of life choices and regrets
  • Beautiful blend of philosophy and fiction
  • Perfect for book club discussions
  • Uplifting message about appreciating life
  • Matt Haig's signature warmth and wisdom

📝 Event Recaps

November Book Discussion: "Klara and the Sun"

November 15, 2024

Our November discussion of Kazuo Ishiguro's "Klara and the Sun" was a thought-provoking exploration of AI consciousness, love, and what it means to be human. The virtual meetup was attended by 45 members who shared diverse perspectives on Klara's unique viewpoint and the novel's themes.

Key Discussion Points:

  • Klara's perspective on human emotions and relationships
  • The symbolism of the sun throughout the novel
  • Ishiguro's exploration of technology and humanity
  • Comparisons to other AI narratives in literature

Author Q&A with Sarah Chen

October 25, 2024

We had the privilege of hosting debut author Sarah Chen for an intimate Q&A session about her thriller "Silent Echoes." Members asked insightful questions about her writing process, research methods, and inspiration for the novel's complex characters.

Highlights:

  • Sarah's journey from idea to publication (3 years!)
  • Behind-the-scenes research on forensic psychology
  • Upcoming projects and sequel possibilities
  • Writing tips for aspiring thriller authors
